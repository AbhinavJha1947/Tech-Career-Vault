# Software Engineer Resume

> [!NOTE]
> This is a placeholder. Please upload your specific resume here (e.g., PDF or DOCX) or paste the markdown content of your resume.

## Key Sections to Include
- **Contact Info**: Name, Email, LinkedIn, GitHub, Portfolio.
- **Summary**: Brief overview of your experience and key skills.
- **Experience**: Reverse chronological order of your work history.
    - Use "STAR" method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for bullet points.
- **Skills**: Languages, Frameworks, Tools, Databases.
- **Education**: Degree, University, Year.
- **Projects**: Notable side projects or open source contributions.
